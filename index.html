Welcome to Cogninut! We are dedicated to creating a nurturing space where children can explore their inner strengths, manage their emotions, and lay the groundwork for a positive and fulfilling future.

Furthermore, we:

  • Develop essential skills, such as attention, effective communication, memory, organization, reasoning, and understanding in children.
  • Foster problem-solving abilities and decision-making skills.
  • Build higher-level cognitive skills for comprehensive development.

Our approach includes:
  • Designing and implementing fun, interactive, and engaging programs.
  • Utilizing psycho-educational methods tailored for children in India.
  • Teaching children how to regulate their emotions and channelize them in a healthy way through psychological interventions.

We focus on


Workshops focus on recognizing emotions, understanding their impact, and developing skills for effective emotional management.

Interactive activities are designed to cultivate empathy, effective communication, and healthy relationships with peers and adults.

Techniques to cope with anxiety and stress are taught, aiding children in adapting to challenging situations and reducing emotional turmoil.
