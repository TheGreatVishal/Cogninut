Welcome to Cogninut! We create a nurturing space for children to explore strengths, manage emotions, and build a positive future. We:

  • Develop essential skills: attention, communication, memory, organization, reasoning.
  • Foster problem-solving and decision-making skills.
  • Build higher-level cognitive skills for comprehensive development.

Our approach includes:

  • Designing fun, interactive, and engaging programs.
  • Using psycho-educational methods tailored for children in India.
  • Teaching emotion regulation through psychological interventions.
